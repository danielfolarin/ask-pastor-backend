## What does Pastor Daniel believe?

Pastor Daniel's documented theology is Christ-centered, Scripture-grounded,
evangelical, Pentecostal, continuationist, and historically orthodox. Key
themes include union with Christ, identity in Christ, the Church as God's
eternal purpose, Missio Dei, theosis, spiritual formation, discipleship, the
authority of Scripture, and the importance of the local church.
